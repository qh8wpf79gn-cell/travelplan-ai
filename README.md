# TravelPlan AI ✈️

**An AI-powered travel itinerary generator that creates personalized, budget-optimized travel plans in seconds.**

🔗 **[Start Planning Now](https://qh8wpf79gn-cell.github.io/travelplan-ai/travelplan_white.html)**

---

## ✨ What It Does

TravelPlan AI uses Claude API to generate detailed, day-by-day travel itineraries tailored to your preferences and budget. No more spending hours researching — get a complete travel plan with logistics, costs, and insider tips in under 30 seconds.

---

## 🎯 Key Features

- **AI-Generated Itineraries**
  - Covers 50+ destinations worldwide
  - Detailed day-by-day plans with activities, restaurants, and logistics
  - Average generation time: 15-30 seconds per itinerary

- **Smart Budget Management**
  - Real-time cost tracking for accommodation, food, and activities
  - Daily budget breakdown with flexible spending suggestions
  - Cost estimation by category (accommodation, dining, attractions, transport)

- **Fully Customizable Travel Style**
  - Choose pace (relaxed, moderate, adventurous)
  - Select accommodation preference (budget hostels, mid-range hotels, luxury resorts)
  - Pick activity types (cultural, adventure, food-focused, nightlife, nature)
  - Dietary preferences and cuisine selection

- **Practical Planning Information**
  - Best time to visit recommendations
  - Transportation logistics between locations
  - Local insider tips and hidden gems
  - Rough timing for each activity

## 🚀 How to Use

### Step 1: Get Your API Key
1. Visit [Anthropic Console](https://console.anthropic.com)
2. Create a free account (includes free credits)
3. Generate your Claude API key

### Step 2: Open the App
- Click **[Start Planning Now](https://qh8wpf79gn-cell.github.io/travelplan-ai/travelplan_white.html)**

### Step 3: Enter Your Details
1. **Paste your API key** into the input field
2. **Choose your destination** (any city or country)
3. **Set your budget** (total budget or daily budget)
4. **Specify duration** (number of days)

### Step 4: Customize Your Style
- **Travel pace:** How fast do you want to move? (Relaxed / Moderate / Adventurous)
- **Accommodation style:** Budget, mid-range, or luxury?
- **Activity preferences:** Cultural sites, outdoor adventures, food tours, nightlife?
- **Dietary restrictions:** Vegetarian, vegan, halal, kosher, gluten-free?

### Step 5: Generate & Enjoy
- Click **Generate Itinerary**
- Wait 15-30 seconds
- Get your complete, personalized travel plan
- Copy, save, or modify as needed
